Welcome to The Happy Harvest: Big Roots, Small Spaces

1. Introduction

"To plant a garden is to believe in tomorrow." - Audrey Hepburn

Grow organic carrots, radishes, beets, onions, potatoes, and turnips right outside your door.

2. Getting Started

  • Pick a sunny spot with 6+ hours of light.
  • Use deep pots with drainage holes.
  • Fill with rich soil and compost.
  • Plant seeds, water gently, and label each pot.

3. Benefits of Container Gardening

  • Fresh, tasty vegetables steps from your kitchen.
  • Lower food costs and less plastic waste.
  • Calming daily routine for all ages.
  • Kids learn where real food comes from.
