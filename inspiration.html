Container Garden Inspiration: Make Every Corner Bloom

1. Layout Inspiration

  • Pick a theme: cottage, color-block, or kitchen-ready patio.
  • Pair root crops with flowers like marigolds for beauty and pollinators.
  • Group pots by height for a calm, layered look.

2. Functional Ideas

  • Add slim trellises behind pots to save floor space.
  • Try vertical planters for herbs above root containers.
  • Host a farm-to-table patio tasting with your harvest.

3. Creative Inspiration

  • Upcycle buckets, crates, or tins into cheerful planters.
  • Paint pots with crop names and harvest dates.
  • Compost scraps and decorate with stones or branches.
